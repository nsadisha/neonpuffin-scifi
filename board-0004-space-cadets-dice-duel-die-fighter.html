Space Cadets: Dice Duel - Die Fighter

Space Cadets: Dice Duel - Die Fighter, the first major expansion to the board game Space Cadets: Dice Duel, opens up a range of completely new experiences to the Space Cadets: Dice Duel universe. Veteran Space Cadets will now have a number of entirely new ways to play:

☆ Fighters can be now flown by a single pilot and are very fast and highly manoeuvrable, but lack shields and can be easily destroyed. There are 3 principal modes of the game:
• In ‘Carrier Strike’ mode of the game (for 6-10 players), each team gets one original starship (now called a Capital Ship) and, additionally, one Fighter to protect it.
• ‘Fighter Assault’ mode (designed for 4-6 players) has two Fighters teaming up and trying to take out a single heavily armed Capital Ship.
• Finally, ‘Fighter Duel’ mode brings the Dice Duel experience to two players, as two Fighters go head-to-head for a quick and ruthless dogfight.

☆ Experimental Equipment Cards upgrade the Capital Ships with a variety of unique and powerful capabilities. Through a draft method, each side selects two upgrades and needs to tailor their strategy to take full advantage of their respective distinct strengths. The 27 Experimental Equipment Cards include enhancements to the existing systems, new weapons, new movement options, and much more.

Space Cadets: Dice Duel - Die Fighter board game contains these components:

◈  27 Experimental Equipment Cards

◈  2 Plastic Fighter Miniatures

◈  22 Custom Engraved Dice

◈  2 Fighter Cockpits

◈  1 Rulebook

Note: Requires the original Space Cadets: Dice Duel board game.

This board game is for 2-10 players (ages 12+) and sells for $35 (£23/€30).
