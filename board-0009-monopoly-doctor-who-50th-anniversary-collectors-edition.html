Monopoly - Doctor Who 50th Anniversary Collector’s Edition

Travel through time and space with the Doctor Who 50th Anniversary Collector’s Edition of Monopoly as you buy, sell and trade iconic episodes, spaceships and landmarks spanning billions of years across the Doctor Who universe. The Poison Sky, A Good Man Goes to War, The Daleks, and Arc of Infinity are all up for grabs as you set your course for success and vie to own it all.

Custom designed game board and Dalek-themed box art celebrating the 50th Anniversary of this seasoned science fiction television series features all eleven doctors, the best loved companions, and of course, the scariest monsters and the meanest baddies.

The board game features:

22 Locations represented by the most popular episodes of the series

6 Collectible Tokens including: The Sonic Screwdriver, The Seventh Doctor’s Umbrella, The Second Doctor’s Recorder, The Fifth Doctor’s Celery, The Eleventh Doctor’s Bow Tie, The Fourth Doctor’s Scarf.

Special ‘U.N.I.T’ and ‘Gallifrey’ cards (replacing ‘Chance’ and ‘Community Chest’ cards).

This board game is for 2-6 players (ages 8+) and sells for $40 (£25/€32).
