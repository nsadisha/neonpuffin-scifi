Mass Effect

Andromeda-latex.com produces bespoke, made-to-measure latex costumes for cosplay. Their collection includes characters from many well-known fantasy and science fiction franchises, Star Wars being one of them. Particularly impressive is their wide range of the characters and races from Mass Effect universe, including Commander Shepard, Miranda Lawson, Kasumi Goto and Asari, which we found eerily life-like. Every costume is custom made to the client’s measurements and, according to the manufacturer, the intricate patterns and other details will not wear out or rub off over time, as they are ingrained in the latex. Some costumes are also made from phosphorescent latex with the addition of ultraviolet-reactive pigments, so they glow in the dark.
