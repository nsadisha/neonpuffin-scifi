Marvel Legends Infinite Series - Guardians of the Galaxy

Hasbro, in its ‘Marvel Legends Infinite Series’ collection of six 6-inch figures, revealed yet another rendering of the, now-highly-celebrated, Guardians of the Galaxy.

Guardians of the Galaxy are a disparate team of rogues led by brash adventurer Peter Quill a.k.a. Star-Lord and containing Gamora – enigmatic and deadly assassin, Rocket – super-sized-gun-toting intelligent genetically modified raccoon, his best friend Groot – tree-like humanoid with rather limited vocabulary, and Drax the Destroyer – alien warrior with super-human strength in search of vengeance.

The latest Hasbro’s Guardians of the Galaxy-themed action figure series contains the standard members of this spacefaring superhero team, that is: Star-Lord, Gamora, Rocket Raccoon, Groot and Drax, plus two affiliated characters: Iron Man and Nova. Groot’s body parts are split amongst the 6 packs (that contain one of each: torso, 2 hands, 2 legs and, ahem, bum) as a build-a-figure kit, and can be assembled into an imposing, to-scale with other members of the team, 9-inch Groot figurine.

The figures come equipped with their trademark weaponry (Star-Lord with two handguns, Gamora with a sword, Drax with a couple of daggers, and Rocket Raccoon with a super-sized rifle – just the way he likes it). In addition, Star-Lord comes with his obligatory walkman and headphones.


These action figures stand 6 inches (15 cm) tall and sell for around $22 (£14/€20) each.
