San Diego Comic-Con International, 9-12 July 2015


The 2015 San Diego Comic-Con International, a comic and multi-genre entertainment convention, will take place at the San Diego Convention Center in San Diego, California, between Thursday 9th and Sunday 12th July 2015.

The largest convention of its kind in the world, the San Diego Comic-Con International was originally showcasing primarily comic books and science fiction and fantasy related topics, but now covers a much wider range of entertainment elements across virtually all genres and media. The convention frequently attracts more than 130,000 attendees, filling the San Diego Convention Center to its capacity.

This year’s programme includes (literally) hundreds of discussion groups, panels, meetings, signings, screenings, workshops, and other events on dozens of science fiction, fantasy, horror, comics, manga, anime and cosplay-related topics.
