'The Phantom' attack shuttle from Star Wars Rebels

Join the rebel resistance against the evil Empire in The Phantom attack shuttle, as seen in the Star Wars Rebels animated television series. Place young rebel hero Ezra Bridger in the detachable cockpit and store his cadet helmet and blaster in the cargo hold. Fit Ezra’s droid companion C1-10P in the back, close the transparent canopy above Ezra’s head, unfold the shuttle’s wings for the flight mode and launch into battle. Fire the 2-way spring-loaded missile launcher to repel attacks from the front or the rear. After a successful mission, dock The Phantom safely on The Ghost starship (sold separately).


This Lego model features an openable and detachable cockpit with a detailed computer dashboard, top-mounted missile launcher (which can be fired forwards or backwards), hull section with removable roof and space for a droid, fold-out wings, rear cargo compartment and translucent red rocket boosters.

The kit also includes 2 new minifigures: Ezra Bridger armed with a blaster and equipped with a cadet helmet, and C1-10P, the grumpy Astromech droid also known as ‘Chopper’.

This 10 x 19 x 9 cm Lego model sells for about $38 (£24/€30).
