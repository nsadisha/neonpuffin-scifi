Use of cookies

Scifi4Ever website may place cookies on your computer or other device that may contain anonymous information that is associated with you. Cookies are small text files a website uses to recognise users, and are typically placed on your hard disk by a web site you visit. Cookies contain information that can be read later by a web site that issued the cookie to you, or that, in some cases, can be read by a third party domain.

Other than during your visit to a particular web site, cookies are dormant, pose no danger to your computer and contain no viruses or other malicious software.

Cookies may trace information about your use of Scifi4Ever website so that we can provide a seamless and more personalised experience for you.

If you do not wish to have Scifi4Ever website placing and using cookies on your computer or device, you can set your browser preferences to refuse cookies before viewing Scifi4Ever website.

However, if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to use particular interactive elements of Scifi4Ever website that depend on them, such as forum, or some parts of Scifi4Ever website might not work on your computer as intended.

Similarly, when you use the Like or Tweet buttons on our website, a cookie may be sent by the web site you have chosen to share the content with. Scifi4Ever does not have any control over the distribution and use of such cookies. You should always check the relevant third party website for more information about these cookies.


How to control cookies

If you do not want to receive cookies, you can adjust your web browser so that it notifies you when cookies are sent to it or you can refuse cookies altogether. You can also delete cookies that have already been saved on your computer.

For more information about adjusting your browser settings with regards to cookies visit: www.aboutcookies.org, which contains detailed instructions on how to do this on a wide variety of most common desktop browsers.
