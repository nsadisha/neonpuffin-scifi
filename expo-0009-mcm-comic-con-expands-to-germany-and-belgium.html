MCM Comic Con expands to Germany and Belgium

After staging the pop culture events in almost every major city in the United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland, including London, Birmingham, Manchester, Liverpool, Glasgow, Belfast and Dublin, MCM Expo Group is expanding their, increasingly popular, franchise overseas. The two new shows, MCM Hannover Comic Con and MCM Belgium Comic Con, are the first to be launched under the new brand MCM Central.

The first MCM Hannover Comic Con took place at the Hannover Messe between 4-5 June 2016, closely followed by MCM Belgium Comic Con that debuted through 11-12 June 2016 at the Brussels Expo. However, this is likely only the beginning of company’s ambitious expansion plans, as Bryan Cooney, MCM managing director, was quoted saying that “this marks the first stage of our accelerated growth plan for 2016 and beyond”.
