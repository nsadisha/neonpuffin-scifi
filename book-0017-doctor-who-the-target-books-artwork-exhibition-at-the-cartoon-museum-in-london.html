'Doctor Who: The Target Books Artwork' at The Cartoon Museum

The Cartoon Museum in Holborn, London hosts an exhibition of original cover artwork that coincides with the reissue of some classic Doctor Who releases including the first seven Doctors by BBC Books.

In what is the first public display of the original artworks, the exhibition will feature over 30 pieces, including the covers from six of the new reissues, ten covers by Chris Achilleos (from 'The Daleks' through to 'Revenge of the Cybermen'), five covers by Andrew Skilleter (from 'Destiny of the Daleks' through to 'The Mark of the Rani') and other classic and well-known original cover artworks such as by Roy Knipe, Jeff Cummins and David McAlister. The original paintings feature likenesses of many famous actors playing the part of the Doctor over the years, such as Jon Pertwee, Tom Baker and Peter Davison, as well as many of the infamous Doctor Who adversaries such as Daleks and Cybermen.

In the days before DVDs and VHS, the novelisations of Doctor Who TV episodes by Target Books were sometimes the only way in which Doctor Who fans could re-enjoy some of the most iconic stories after they were broadcast on BBC. As a matter of fact, many fans of a certain age first experienced a great number of the Time Lord’s adventures for the first time through print, as opposed to television.

One of such fans is Doctor Who writer and re-imagined Sherlock co-creator Mark Gatiss, who said to BBC:
“To finally see some of these memorable covers in the flesh is a wonderful, unexpected treat. It’s a testament to their terrific, punchy immediacy that they feel both nostalgic and brand new. Show a copy of any one of these glorious novelisations to people of a certain age and they are transported back to a simpler, cosier age.”.

This is the very first time that the cover artwork has been publicly exhibited, so Doctor Who fans have an unique opportunity to finally see the original paintings which became the covers for this memorable range of books by Target Books imprint issued during 1970s and 80s.

Doctor Who: The Target Books Artwork exhibition takes place at The Cartoon Museum, 35 Little Russell Street, Holborn, London (the museum is located just a short walk from either Tottenham Court Road or Holborn underground station) from Thursday 28th April to Sunday 15th May.
