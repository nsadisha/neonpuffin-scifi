Lego Rocket Raccoon (‘Guardians of the Galaxy’ variant)

An expert in weaponry and the combat strategist for the ‘Guardians of the Galaxy’, Rocket, the intelligent, genetically-modified raccoon, may be the only thing standing between the enemies of the galaxy and the total destruction of the known universe.

This Marvel’s ‘Guardians of the Galaxy’ variant Lego Rocket Raccoon minifigure doesn’t sport too much fur on its shiny brick body, but despite that doesn’t fail to pack in a super-sized gun, just the way Rocket likes it.

‘Guardians of the Galaxy’ Rocket Raccoon Lego minifigure features:

 • Highly-stylised Rocket Raccoon minifigure as seen in the movie

 • A bespoke head-mould brick with a Raccoon-like appearance

 • Torso with brown shoulder-pads and print reminiscent of the orange-and-dark-grey space suit worn by Rocket the Raccoon in the ‘Guardians of the Galaxy’ movie

 • Dark grey arms to represent the sleeveless top of the space suit

 • One super-sized gun (just the way Rocket likes it)


This Lego minifigure sells for around $14 (£9/€13).
