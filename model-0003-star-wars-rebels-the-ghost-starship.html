'The Ghost' starship from Star Wars Rebels

Get ready to battle the Empire with the rebel flagship - The Ghost - as seen in the Star Wars Rebels animated television series. Stay on course with rebel minifigures in two cockpits and put Zeb on the lookout in the 360-degree rotating gun turret. Land The Phantom attack shuttle (sold separately) in the docking area at the rear. Attack the enemy with 2 spring-loaded missile launchers and then reload the spare missiles from the missile storage. In an emergency, hurry to the escape pods and eject!

This Lego model features 2 cockpits (front cockpit is detachable) with seats for minifigures, 360-degree rotating gun turret with elevating guns, 2 spring-loaded missile launchers, 2 detachable escape pods, a cargo hatch with a release handle, cargo container holding a Holocron, 2 hatches for storage of spare missiles, 2 huge rocket boosters and a docking area that can accommodate The Phantom attack shuttle model, which is sold separately.

The kit also includes 4 new minifigures: Kanan Jarrus, Hera Syndulla, Garazeb ‘Zeb’ Orrelios and a Stormtrooper, armed with assorted weapons including lightsaber, blaster handgun, blaster rifle and a Zeb’s collapsible bo-rifle, transformable into a bo-staff for close combat.

This 28 x 54 x 9 cm Lego model sells for about $90 (£56/€71).
