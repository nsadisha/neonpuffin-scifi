Star Wars - 501st Legion - Slovakia Outpost

The 501st Legion (aka “Vader's Fist”) is an international fan-based organisation dedicated to the construction and wearing of screen-accurate replicas of Imperial Stormtrooper armour, as well as costumes of Sith Lords, Clone Troopers, Bounty Hunters, and other villains from the Star Wars universe. The 501st Legion, made up entirely of volunteers, now has over 6,500 active members worldwide, with nearly 11,000 costumes among them. The Legion is active on 5 continents, with Garrisons and Outposts in over 50 countries around the globe. Slovakia Outpost, whose members can be seen below, is one of the youngest yet most active and creative units of the 501st Legion.
