Guardians of the Galaxy 'Battle Gear' 2 Packs

Hasbro’s new range of twelve 2 ½" action figures (9 unique figures with 3 characters included twice) from Guardians of the Galaxy probably won’t win any quality awards, but then, they come at just $3 for a figure and $30 buys you the whole series of 12 action figures.


Drax & Korath

Hero meets villain in this Guardians of the Galaxy figure 2-pack. The pack includes not only vengeful destroyer Drax, but also his frightening nemesis, Korath. They come with a missile launcher, weapons and other battle gear that will make their fight a universe-shaking event. Who will win in the end is all up to you.



     Features and accessories:

     • Drax and Korath action figures

     • Rocket Launcher that fires missiles

     • Outfitable with the attached battle gear and weapons


Star-Lord & Gamora

There is a dynamic duo in this Guardians of the Galaxy figure 2-pack. The pack includes not only intergalactic adventurer Star-Lord, but also his assassin ally, Gamora. They come with a missile launcher, weapons and other battle gear that will make their fight against interstellar evil a cosmos-shaking event. Who will win in the end is all up to you.



     Features and accessories:

     • Star-Lord and Gamora action figures

     • Rocket Launcher that fires missiles

     • Outfitable with the attached battle gear and weapons


Ronan & Star-Lord

Hero meets villain in this Guardians of the Galaxy figure 2-pack. The pack includes not only intergalactic adventurer Star-Lord, but also his frightening nemesis, Ronan. They come with a missile launcher, weapons and other battle gear that will make their fight a space-shaking event. Who will win in the end is all up to you.



     Features and accessories:

     • Ronan and Star-Lord action figures

     • Rocket Launcher that fires missiles

     • Outfitable with the attached battle gear and weapons


Groot & Rocket Raccoon + Nova Corps Officer

There is a powerful trio in this Guardians of the Galaxy figure 3-pack. The pack includes not only tree-like humanoid Groot and his tiny-yet-oversized-gun-toting ally Rocket Raccoon, but also a soldier for their cause, Nova Corps Officer. They come with a missile launcher, weapons and other battle gear that will make their fight against interstellar evil a cosmos-shaking event. Who will win in the end is all up to you.



     Features and accessories:

     • Groot, Rocket Raccoon and Nova Corps Officer action figures

     • Rocket Launcher that fires missiles

     • Outfitable with the attached battle gear and weapons


Groot & Rocket Raccoon + Sakaaran Trooper

Formidable duo of heroes meets villain in this Guardians of the Galaxy figure 3-pack. The pack includes not only tree-like humanoid Groot and his tiny-yet-oversized-gun-wielding ally Rocket Raccoon, but also their frightening nemesis, Sakaaran Trooper. They come with a missile launcher, weapons and other battle gear that will make their fight a galaxy-shaking event. Who will win in the end is all up to you.



     Features and accessories:

     • Groot, Rocket Raccoon and Sakaaran Trooper action figures

     • Rocket Launcher that fires missiles

     • Outfitable with the attached battle gear and weapons

These 2 ½" figures with 5 points-of-articulation require some very basic assembly, sell for $6 (£4/€5) for a pack of 2 (or 3 in the last two packs) and are compatible with Hasbro’s Guardians of the Galaxy ‘Pursuit Spacecraft Vehicles’ (Rocket Raccoon Warbird, Milano Starship, Nova Corps Starblaster, Necrocraft).
