Executive Officer Kane from Alien

Alien made audiences jump out of their seats in 1979 and played a major role in cementing science fiction film as a widely popular genre. Among the intricate set and realistic spaceship design work, the costumes contributed greatly to the film’s both critical acclaim and box office success. The environmental protection suit is the focus of this resin model kit of Executive Officer Kane. The resin parts capture every minute detail of the astronaut down to the quilted pattern of his space suit. The model focuses on the tension-filled moment when Kane first encounters the bizarre Alien life form in its embryonic stage and kneels down to examine the Alien egg. The figure is engineered in a way that allows for realistic lighting effects and rests on a detailed base. The kit also includes decals and an illustrated instruction sheet.

This first all-new Alien model in years captures the iconic moment in the film just seconds before a facehugger bursts out of the egg, melts through Kane’s helmet visor, and attaches itself to his face.

The 1/9 scale model requires CA glue and paint for full assembly and retails for $135 (£84/€107).
