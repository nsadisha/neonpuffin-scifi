Vigilante Rick Grimes from ’The Walking Dead’ TV series

A former sheriff deputy, Rick Grimes leads the group of Atlanta survivors in their desperate search for a safe haven from the constant dangers that now lurk behind every corner in this new, unrecognisable, desolate America. As the group struggles to preserve their bare lives in the unforgiving post-apocalyptic landscape, Rick initially resists his destined leadership role. But as he discovers that there is more than just the relentless hordes of undead to worry about, Rick makes the ultimate choice to do whatever it takes to keep his family and friends alive in this lawless post-civilization world.

McFarlane Toys’ Rick Grimes Vigilante Edition deluxe figure depicts this transformation from an unassuming peace-keeping deputy to a judge, jury and executioner. This blood-splattered 10-inch figure features the spitting-image likeness of the main character of the show, taken from Andrew Lincoln’s full-body 3D scan.

The figure comes packed in a deluxe box containing authentic, to-scale replicas of Rick’s signature weapons: long-barrelled revolver, machete with red handle, knife and a pair of carabiners joined by a chain. The kit also includes two interchangeable arms with differently shaped hands that enable to strike additional character poses with various weapon combinations.

The television series-accurate Rick Grimes collectible figure features:

 ● Authentic and highly detailed likeness of Rick Grimes as appearing in ‘The Walking Dead’ television horror drama series

 ● TV Series-accurate, blood-stained facial expression

 ● Body with multiple points of articulation

 ● A pair of interchangeable arms including:

    • One hand with a partially open palm (for holding a machete)

    • One hand with a bent index finger (for holding a revolver)

 ● Rick’s signature weapons and gear including:

    • Blood-splattered machete with red handle

    • Long-barrelled revolver

    • Black revolver holster

    • Blood-stained knife

    • Pair of carabiners hanging on a chain

This articulated 25cm (10-inch) tall TV series-accurate figure sells for around $45 (£32/€40).
