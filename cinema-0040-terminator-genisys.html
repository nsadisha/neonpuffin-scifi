Terminator Genisys

The Terminator: Genisys trailer has been released by Paramount Pictures on 4 December 2014.

Terminator: Genisys will be the fifth instalment in the Terminator series, and is said to "reset" the series, starting what will be a total reboot of the franchise, with two more films already confirmed for 2017 and 2018.

The veteran of the franchise Arnold Schwarzenegger will return as Terminator (original T-800 model), and will appear in the film alongside the newcomers Emilia Clarke (Daenerys Targaryen in the cult fantasy television series ‘Game of Thrones’) as Sarah Connor, Jason Clarke as John Connor, Jai Courtney as Kyle Reese and Lee Byung-Hun as the shapeshifting T-1000. Matt Smith (Eleventh Doctor in the cult British science fiction television series ‘Doctor Who’) will also star in one of the major roles.

After less-than-lukewarm reception of the fourth instalment of the series, Terminator Salvation (Schwarzenegger himself called the film “awful”), the larger-than-life star of the first three Terminator films agreed to reprise his role as the titular character (no doubt an uber-fat cheque played some role in this decision) and (hopefully) return some oomph to the ailing franchise.

Prior to the filming, David Ellison, producer of the movie, had several meetings with James Cameron, where they discussed Arnold Schwarzenegger's role and how to stay true to the original T-800 character. Cameron pointed out that the outer tissue of the Terminator was actually not synthetic, but organic, and therefore would age in a same way as a body of a normal human being would, and that this could give an excellent cover story for (now also substantially aged) Arnold Schwarzenegger’s return.

Terminator Genisys is scheduled to be released on 1 July 2015.
