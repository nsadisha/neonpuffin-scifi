Loncon 3 - Masks in the crowd

Before the official masquerade even began, the corridors of ExCeL, hosting the 72nd World Science Fiction Convention known as Loncon 3, were teeming with all sorts of superheroes, monsters, aliens, movie superstars, anime characters and supernatural beings. You could stop for a quick chat with Captain Jack Sparrow of the ‘Pirates of the Caribbean’ fame on your way to the next programme event, bump into No-Face from ‘Spirited Away’ while buying your snack or get spooked by the sinister Slender Man while heading towards the bathroom.
