‘Rocket Raccoon Warbird’ from Guardians of the Galaxy

Relive the memorable moments from Guardians of the Galaxy with this Hasbro model toy ‘Rocket Raccoon Warbird’ from the ‘Pursuit Spacecraft Vehicles Wave 1’ series.

To Rocket the Raccoon, happiness comes in the shape of his Warbird starfighter's control stick as he zigs and zags through blistering explosions of plasma, avoiding enemy fire. When the right moment comes, the wings of his starfighter can be pivoted up into battle mode to access an arsenal of weapons that puts most stellar battleships to shame

The kit includes spaceship that features a reveal battle mode, missile launcher with one missile, upgradeable battle gear and comes with a 2 ½" scale Rocket Raccoon pilot figure from the film that can be placed into the cockpit. Some simple assembly is required.

All Pursuit Spacecraft vehicles are compatible with the assorted 2 ½" scale action figures in the Battle Gear 2 Pack line (sold separately).

This Hasbro toy model measures approximately 29 x 19 x 8 cm and sells for about $15 (£10/€12).
