Commander Shepard from the ‘Mass Effect’ video game

A veteran soldier of the Systems Alliance Navy military, N7-graduate of the Interplanetary Combatives Training (ICT) programme, and the first human Spectre of the Citadel Council, Lieutenant Commander Shepard dedicates his life to stopping ‘The Reapers’ – a hostile synthetic alien race hell-bent on wiping out all organic life in the galaxy.

The fully articulated figure based on Commander Shepard’s look in ‘Mass Effect 3’ includes:


Authentic and highly detailed likeness of Commander Shepard as appearing in BioWare's ‘Mass Effect 3’ video game with these features:


●  Removable mask on the helmet, which, when removed, reveals the lower

    part of Commander Shepard’s face just under the visor

●  Highly realistic body with multiple points of articulation

●  Three pairs of interchangeable hands including:

     • One pair with relaxed, open palms

     • One pair with partially open palms

       (for holding a gun, a rifle, or an omni-blade)

     • One pair of hands closed into fists


●  The figure comes with three highly detailed weapons:

     • M8 Avenger

     • M5 Phalanx

     • Omni-blade / Omni-tool


This video game-accurate 1/6 scale collectible figure stands 12 inches (30 cm) tall and sells for around $220 (£145/€200).
