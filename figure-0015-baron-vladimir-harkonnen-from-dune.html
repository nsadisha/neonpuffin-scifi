Baron Vladimir Harkonnen from the new Dune movie

American manufacturer of action figures McFarlane Toys comes with this highly-realistic, ultra-articulated, 12-inches (30cm) tall figurine of Baron Vladimir Harkonnen as portrayed by Stellan Skarsgård in the 2021 Denis Villeneuve film Dune.

The very personification of evil, Baron Vladimir Harkonnen is a gluttonous force of malevolence, ever ready to feed his insatiable addiction to brutality. A gravity–defying blob of a man, gliding through the air like a phantom of cruelty, the all-consuming figurehead of House Harkonnen rules through fear and violence, determined to take back control of the most valuable planet in the entire universe – the planet whose custodian is even more influential and powerful than whoever sits on the Imperial throne – and reclaim the Melange-rich sands of Arrakis from the House Atreides by any means necessary, even if that means using intrigue and murder.

The Baron Vladimir Harkonnen collectible action figure features:

 ● The Baron hovering menacingly in the air supported by his anti-gravity suspensors

 ● Incredibly detailed 12” scale figure based on the 2021 motion picture Dune

 ● Figure features ultra-articulation with up to 22 moving parts for full range of posing

 ● Figure comes with a base and with various character-specific accessories

 ● Figure is showcased in bespoke Dune–themed window box packaging

This fully-articulated 30cm (12-inch) tall movie-accurate figurine sells for around $55 (£40/€45) and is available now from Amazon and also from assorted toy-specialised retailers. This figure-line (a.k.a. Wave 1) contains five additional figures to collect: Paul Atreides, Lady Jessica, Duncan Idaho, Stilgar and Glossu ‘Beast’ Rabban, all realistically modelled on their respective movie counterparts – Timothée Chalamet, Rebecca Ferguson, Jason Momoa, Javier Bardem and Dave Bautista. Some of the important main characters that didn’t make it into Wave 1 – Duke Leto Atreides (Oscar Isaac), Chani (Zendaya) and Gurney Halleck (Josh Brolin) are likely to be released amongst Wave 2 figures.
