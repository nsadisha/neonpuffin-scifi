MCM Comic Con – London, October 2014

London MCM Comic Con (or MCM Expo) is a multi-genre fan convention held in ExCeL, Royal Victoria Dock, London, twice yearly, usually on the last weekend of May and October. The MCM Comic Con is organised by the MCM Expo Group.

This year won’t be an exception and the convention will take place on the last full weekend of October, i.e. from Friday 24th to Sunday 26th of October 2014.

The convention mainly focuses on comics, anime, manga, fantasy and sci-fi genres, as well as video games, mainly from the United Kingdom and Japan, but also from other countries from all around the world.

London MCM Comic Con features a large floor space for corporate exhibitors, which include well-known movie studios and TV stations, as well as a separate section for private exhibitors and assorted dealer stalls. There is also a theatre with a large stage equipped with projection monitors and seating for audience of 1000.

The convention is frequently attended by movie and television show stars as well as various memorable industry personalities and celebrities.

The highlights of the October’s Expo will be: Special Guests (see below), Photo with the Stars Area, Video Games Expo (with BandaiNamco, Capcom, Koei Tecmo, NISA and Tecmo Koei confirmed), ESL eSports (with qualifying tournaments for League of Legends), Pop Asia (fusion of Japanese, Korean & other Asian cultures), Comic Village (Workshop for Comic Creators and Fans alike), Cosplay & Masquerade (with EuroCosplay Championship Finals), Steampunk, Vidfest UK (an event for fans of online video and web series), Theatre (with previews of upcoming feature films and television shows), Retro Gaming, Stalls with Memorabilia and Dealer Stalls.

On the Saturday evening of each London MCM Comic Con is held The MCM Expo Party with a chance to get together, dance, eat and drink (the October’s party takes place at Novotel London ExCeL).

Special Guests:

★ Daniel Radcliffe (with a promotional signing of his new movie Horns)

★ Robert Picardo (starring in Star Trek, Stargate, Supernatural, Pushing Daisies and Smallville amongst others)

★ Shinichiro Watanabe (directing The Animatrix)

★ Dylan Sprayberry (Man of Steel)

★ D. C. Douglas (voice of Albert Wesker in Resident Evil 5 and Legion in Mass Effect 2)

★ Cast & Crew of ‘Welcome To Purgatory’, and many others.

Daniel Radcliffe, Robert Picardo, Shinichiro Watanabe and Mackenzie Lintz are only some of the celebrities attending London MCM Comic Con this October.
