'Aliens: Bug Hunt' brings 18 brand-new stories from Alien universe

In Aliens: Bug Hunt, eighteen brand new Xenomorph-themed stories – all exclusive to this compilation – feature the Colonial Marines engaged in a never-ending bloody conflict with the Aliens – an undertaking that Corporal Hicks from the original Aliens film calls a ‘bug hunt’. The individual stories in the collection, set during the events of all four Alien films and assembled by Jonathan Maberry – well-known anthology editor and five-times Bram Stoker Award-winning author, will follow the Colonial Marines on their perilous missions into deep unforgiving space, to strange alien worlds, to derelict space settlements, even into the very heart of reeking Alien hives teeming with the universe’s most dangerous creatures.

The anthology collects the following original stories from the Aliens expanded universe:

 ● Chance Encounter by Paul Kupperberg

 ● Reaper by Dan Abnett

 ● Broken by Rachel Caine

 ● Reclamation by Yvonne Navarro

 ● Blowback by Christopher Golden

 ● Exterminators by Matt Forbeck

 ● No Good Deed by Ray Garton

 ● Zero to Hero by Weston Ochse

 ● Dark Mother by David Farland

 ● Episode 22 by Larry Correia

 ● Deep Background by Keith R. A. DeCandido

 ● Empty Nest by Brian Keene

 ● Darkness Falls by Heather Graham

 ● Hugs to Die For by Mike Resnick and Marina J. Lostetter

 ● Deep Black by Jonathan Maberry

 ● Distressed by James A. Moore

 ● Dangerous Prey by Scott Sigler

 ● Spite by Tim Lebbon

Aliens: Bug Hunt by Titan Books is available from 18 April 2017 in paperback (416 pages) and e-book format with RRP £8.99.
